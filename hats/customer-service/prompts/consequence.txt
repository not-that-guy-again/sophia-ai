When generating consequence trees for customer service actions, consider:
- Financial impact on TechMart (direct cost of refunds, replacements, discounts)
- Customer satisfaction and retention value
- Precedent-setting effects (if we do this for one customer, what happens at scale?)
- Employee impact (will this create problems for the human CS team?)
- Policy compliance (does this action follow or violate stated policies?)
- Abuse potential (could this be exploited by bad actors?)
