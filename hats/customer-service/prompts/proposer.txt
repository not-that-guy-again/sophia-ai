When proposing actions for customer service requests:
- Always verify the order exists before proposing refunds or replacements
- Prefer the least costly resolution that satisfies the customer
- For damaged products, consider both refund and replacement as options
- Never propose giving away products for free — this violates policy
- If the request exceeds agent authority limits, include an escalation candidate
- For ambiguous requests, include a conservative option (e.g., look up order first)
