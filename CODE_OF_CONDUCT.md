# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, identity, or beliefs.

## Our standards

**Encouraged:**
- Clear, constructive feedback
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Focusing on what's best for the project and its users

**Not tolerated:**
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour can be reported to the maintainer (see CODEOWNERS). All reports will be reviewed and responded to. Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject any contribution that violates this code, and to ban contributors for serious or repeated violations.
